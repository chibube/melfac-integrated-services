What We Do

End-to-End Trade & Logistics Services

From pre-shipment documentation to final delivery, Melfac handles every stage of your import process with precision and regulatory expertise.

🛃

Customs Clearing & Documentation

Full NCS-licensed customs clearance at all major Nigerian seaports and airports. Form M processing, duty assessment, examination management, and final release from lodgement to delivery.

Core Service
✈️

Air & Sea Freight Forwarding

International freight forwarding for FCL, LCL, and air cargo from any origin. We coordinate shipping lines, airlines, and inland haulage to ensure your cargo moves on time and in full.

Freight
💊

NAFDAC Fast Track Clearance

Specialist clearance for pharmaceutical, food, cosmetic, and regulated products. We leverage our direct NAFDAC working relationship to facilitate fast track inspection and release.

Regulatory
🏷️

SONCAP & SON Compliance

Pre-shipment product conformity assessment for all SON-regulated products including electrical equipment, generators, and engineering components. Valid SONCAP certificates, guaranteed.

Compliance
📋

IDEC Processing

Import Duty Exemption Certificate (IDEC) processing for qualifying imports. We identify eligible shipments and liaise with relevant ministries to reduce your duty liability on every qualifying consignment.

Cost Reduction
⭐

AEO Advisory & Processing

Authorised Economic Operator (AEO) advisory and application support. AEO status unlocks Green and Blue channel clearance — reducing clearance time from weeks to days at Nigerian ports.

Advisory
⚙️

Oil & Gas Project Logistics

Specialised logistics for oil and gas including project cargo, oversized equipment, and sensitive consignments. Deep experience with NUPRC/DPR documentation and operations at Onne Port.

Specialist
🚢

Vessel TIP Clearance

Temporary Importation Permit (TIP) clearance for vessels and marine equipment. Full NCS process for vessel entries, ensuring compliance for offshore and maritime operations.

Maritime
🏪

Warehousing & Distribution

Bonded and open warehousing in Lagos with flexible storage and distribution options. Post-clearance logistics including last-mile delivery coordination across Nigeria.

Logistics
🛂

Crew Change & Expatriate Immigration

Immigration and documentation support for crew change and expatriate entry into Nigeria. STR visas, CERPAC processing, and all NIS requirements for international personnel.

Why Choose Us

The Melfac Advantage

Our 17-year track record is built on regulatory expertise, institutional relationships, and a genuine commitment to reducing the cost and complexity of importing into Nigeria.

  • 01

    NCS Licensed & Fully Regulated

    Melfac holds a current NCS licence — a mandatory requirement for customs agents in Nigeria. We operate fully within the regulatory framework, protecting your cargo and your business.

  • 02

    NAFDAC & Regulatory Network

    Direct working relationships with NAFDAC, SON, and other agencies allow us to fast-track clearance for pharmaceutical, food, and regulated products — turning weeks into days.

  • 03

    Free HS Code Review & Duty Optimisation

    Complimentary pre-shipment HS code classification review and duty estimate for every new client. We eliminate the risk of misclassification — the leading cause of port disputes in Nigeria.

  • 04

    Origin-Country Support

    Our offices in Lagos, London, and Houston allow us to review documentation at source — ensuring it is correct before cargo departs, eliminating costly corrections at Nigerian entry.

Every new shipment gets a complimentary pre-shipment HS code review and duty estimate. Misclassification causes more port holds than any other single factor — we eliminate that risk before cargo departs.

⚡

Green & Blue Channel Access

Through our AEO advisory, clients bypass physical examination queues — reducing clearance time from weeks to days at all major Nigerian ports.

💰

IDEC — Duty Reduction

We proactively identify IDEC-eligible shipments — directly reducing duty liability for qualifying imports. Money back into your landed cost on every eligible shipment.

🌍

Origin-Country Support

Our offices in London and Houston review your documentation at source before cargo ships — eliminating corrections and rejections at the Nigerian port of entry.

🤝

Regulatory Relationships

📊

Real-time status updates, full duty computation breakdowns, and post-clearance cost reports for every consignment. You always know where your cargo is and what it cost.

About Melfac

Built in Nigeria. Built for Nigeria.

Founded in Lagos in 2008 and NCS-licensed since inception, Melfac Integrated Services Limited has built its reputation on one principle: your cargo clears on time, at the right cost, with zero regulatory surprises.

17 Years of Moving
Nigeria Forward

Founded in Lagos in 2008, Melfac was built by Nigerians who understood one thing that foreign clearing agents never could: the Nigerian port system requires relationships, presence, and institutional credibility — not just paperwork.

Over 17 years, we have cleared cargo for some of Nigeria's most demanding importers — pharmaceutical companies, oil and gas operators, FMCG distributors, and engineering firms — delivering on time even when others couldn't.
